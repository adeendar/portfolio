Background

Red Canary Song, a grassroots migrant collective of Asian sex workers organizing in NYC and transationally, came to our team with a question, why are Asian massage workers still being policed for their work inspite of the demcriminalization of sex work in NYC in 2021?

As part of this project, I was tasked with designing the front-end design and development of this digitial mapping project that is available at bdoyworkersatlas.com ↗

Oral History Integration
